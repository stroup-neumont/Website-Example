My Journey

Welcome! I'm Jeremy Stroup, a senior product development leader and professor at Neumont College of Computer Science. My passion lies in technology and product development connecting theoretical knowledge and practical application

With years of experience in both industry and academia, I bring real-world insights to my classes. I believe in hands-on learning where students don't just memorize concepts but build actual projects that showcase their creativity and technical skills.

The CSC210 class covers an introduction to HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and APIs. We'll explore modern web standards, semantic HTML, responsive design, and interactive JavaScript features. Currently, we're diving into APIs and JSON/XML and, if time permits, look at React and web dev tools

Teaching Philosophy

I believe every person learns differently. Everyone learns by doing. I try to diversify teaching activities with lectures, hands-on projects, real-world scenarios, collaborative learning, and multimedia. Every student has unique potential, and my role is to help them discover and develop their natural characteristics
